Death by Design (2017)

Caged Magpie Productions presents its 6th short film, Death by Design, a story that unfolds in a dream-like narration of a young woman, lost within our modern world. Capturing the heightened essence of naivety, we witness Julianne (played by Marley Hamilton) coated with a tainted and unusual upbringing. She now finds herself disoriented and confused within this misconceived society.

Founded in 2012, Caged Magpie Productions is an independent production company that’s based in Bristol and South Wales. Our work ranges from short films, documentaries including sound documentaries, music videos, music scores and many other filmmakingmediums.

Our aim is to aid the independent creatives who are fresh to the industry as well as continuously generating new and unique work. Everyone at Caged Magpie Productions is proud to share the same beliefs of keeping all our productions professional, personal and pour our undivided attention into each production.

We are always open to collaboration and are keen to meet new and upcoming filmmakers, whatever your skillset, so, please don’t ever hesitate to get in touch below.
