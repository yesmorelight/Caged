Displaced (2013)

A Caged Magpie Production Presents Displaced, a story that tells the startlingly original and brutal story of a young couple who once achieved what they mostly desired and then lost it. Brendon (Elliot Winter) and Alex (Jasmine Atkins-Smart) are frozen in this numb reality with no hope of moving forward. Isolated from friends and family by their own actions can a young, naive couple break the ties of their tragic past and rebuild what they once had.
